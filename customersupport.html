How to Order

Web Site Ordering

Step 1: Each employee will be required to insert Employee Number in the User ID box and the Employee Password where it is requested.

Step 2: Your company provided allowance can now be spent on any uniform items available by clicking on a product category from which you wish to order. At any time you can view your allowance balance and personal purchase information at the top of the screen. To purchase a specific item, click on the product image, select the desired size, and then click on the "Add To Shopping Cart" button. When the Order Summary screen appears you may continue shopping by clicking on one of the product categories. Please remember when ordering pants an inseam length is required.

Step 3: Once you have completed your order, click on the "Checkout" button. Please add your Customer Shipping Information before clicking the "Continue" button... If you do not have an e-mail address to receive an order confirmation, we suggest you print a copy of your order before submission. Keep a copy of your order for your records.

Step 4: The next step will be to confirm your itemized order. This will also be the last opportunity to make any additions or changes to your order. If you are satisfied with your uniform order, click the "Submit" button and your order will be processed.

How to measure for your uniforms

General Information

Measure the employee accurately so that the uniform will fit properly. Hold the tape measure firmly, but not tightly, and be sure it is straight and level. Record measurements on the Uniform Requisition.

Measuring Male Employees

Measure male employees by following these steps:

Height - Have the employee stand straight against a wall in stocking feet. Make a mark level with the top of the head. Measure from this point down to the floor.

Chest - Make sure the employee is standing in a natural position. Measure employee well up under arms across shoulder blades and over fullest part.

Waist - Measure over shirt (but not over trousers) at the position the employee normally wears his trousers, or measure the waistband of a well-fitting pair of slacks (zipper up and closed) of the same style.

Neck - Measure around the neck where a buttoned shirt would fit. Be sure tape measure is neither tight enough to choke nor loose enough to sag.

Sleeve - Measure from the middle of the back of the neck, across the shoulders, and around the point of the elbow to the wrist bone.

Inseam - Measure well - fitting slacks of the same style along the inner seam from the crotch seam to bottom of leg.

Payment Information

All purchases that exceed your uniform allowance will require a personal payment at the time of the order. If you use your credit card, charges will not be processed until your garments are shipped.
